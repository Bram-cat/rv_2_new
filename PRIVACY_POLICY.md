# Privacy Policy for Speechi

**Last Updated:** February 9, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Speechi ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information

- **Account Information:** Email address, name (optional), and password (encrypted)
- **Authentication Data:** Managed securely through Clerk authentication services

### 2. Audio Recordings

- **Voice Data:** Audio recordings of your speech practice sessions
- **Recording Metadata:** Duration, date, time, and session information
- **Audio files are processed for analysis and stored securely**

### 3. Speech Analysis Data

- **Transcriptions:** Text versions of your audio recordings generated via AssemblyAI
- **Performance Metrics:** Speaking rate, pause patterns, filler word usage, pitch variation
- **AI Feedback:** Coaching insights and recommendations generated through OpenAI
- **Practice History:** Your progress over time, scores, and improvement metrics

### 4. Usage Information

- **App Activity:** Features used, session duration, and interaction patterns
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, app version
- **Error Logs:** Technical data to improve app performance

### 5. Subscription Information

- **Payment Processing:** Managed through RevenueCat and app store platforms (Apple App Store, Google Play)
- **Subscription Status:** Plan type, renewal dates, purchase history

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide Services:**
   - Analyze your speech recordings
   - Generate personalized feedback and coaching
   - Track your progress and improvement
   - Provide transcriptions of your recordings

2. **Improve User Experience:**
   - Personalize coaching recommendations
   - Enhance app features and functionality
   - Fix bugs and technical issues

3. **Account Management:**
   - Authenticate your identity
   - Manage your subscription
   - Send important service updates

4. **Legal Compliance:**
   - Comply with legal obligations
   - Protect our rights and prevent fraud

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

- All data is transmitted using secure HTTPS encryption
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- Audio files are stored securely with access controls
- Regular security audits and updates

### Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data is retained while your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts:** Data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Recordings:** You can delete individual recordings at any time
- **Backups:** Deleted data may remain in backups for up to 90 days

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

1. **Access Your Data:** View all personal information we hold about you
2. **Delete Your Data:** Request deletion of your account and all associated data
3. **Export Your Data:** Download your recordings and analysis data
4. **Opt-Out:** Skip authentication and use the app without an account (limited features)
5. **Modify Your Data:** Update your account information at any time

### How to Exercise Your Rights

- **Delete Account:** Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
- **Delete Recordings:** Long-press any recording and select "Delete"
- **Export Data:** Contact us at vsbharaniram5@gmail.com
- **Data Requests:** Email us at vsbharaniram5@gmail.com
